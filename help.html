Frequently Asked Questions

How do I evaluate a student's performance?

To evaluate a student, go to the Evaluation section, enter the student's name and scores for each subject, and click the "Evaluate" button. The system will generate a detailed performance report with insights.

Can I watch recorded lectures on SEMS?

Yes! The Lecture Videos section contains all recorded lectures organized by subject and semester. You can search for specific topics or browse by category.

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it in your inbox.

How can I track my academic progress?

The Evaluation section provides detailed analytics on your performance. You can view subject-wise scores, overall grades, and progress over time through interactive charts.

Are my personal details secure on SEMS?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All personal information is encrypted, and we comply with educational data protection regulations. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I download my evaluation reports?

Yes, in the Evaluation section, after generating a report, you'll see a "Download Report" button. You can save it as a PDF for your records.
